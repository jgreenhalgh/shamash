Die Vision

We aim to radically simplify residential solar installations by compiling all necessary information for interested homeowners in a easy-to-understand manner. Customers only have to mark their roof on a satellite image built into our website and provide us with some basic data related to their house. From there on our intuitive process guides the customer from evaluating the costs and benefits of a solar power plant to requesting a quote from a peer-rated installer of his choice.

We want to help Switzerland make the transition to a renewable energy-based economy.
